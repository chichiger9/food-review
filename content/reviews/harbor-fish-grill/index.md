![Catch of the day](./cover.svg)

The menu is short on purpose. I ordered the grilled catch with vegetables and a lemon wedge—cooked through but still moist, with a little char on the skin. Sides were fresh, not an afterthought.

![Dining room](./interior.svg)

Reservations help on weekends. If you can, sit facing the water; it is half the reason to go.
