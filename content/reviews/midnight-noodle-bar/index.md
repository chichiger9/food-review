restaurant: Midnight Noodle Bar

---

![Bowl at the bar](./cover.svg)

I showed up just before last call and still got a full bowl of noodles with enough chew to feel handmade. The broth leans savory and a little sweet—comfort food without trying too hard. I would come back for the dumplings alone.

Price felt fair for the neighborhood. Service was quick; the room is small and a bit loud, but that matches the late-night energy.
